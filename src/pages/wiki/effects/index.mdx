Effects
=======

Overview
--------

Bitwig Studio includes two main types of effects devices:

- [Audio FX](audio-fx)
- [Note FX](note-fx)

[Note FX](note-fx) apply to the [MIDI](midi) notes coming from a single device, while
[Audio FX](audio-fx) can be applied either at the level of an individual track, or to
multiple tracks by using an [Effect Track](effect-track).
