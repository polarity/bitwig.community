# Monitor bus

Use Treemonster as shown in the video below. The trick is setting the "red dot" just below and to the left of the lowest fundamental pitches of the audio signal. (Some pre-filtering might help.) The other trick is setting the **Ring** knob all the way OFF (counter-clockwise).
